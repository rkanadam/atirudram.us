Overview of Daily Events

  • 11 homa kundas will be setup for the sacred fire ceremony
  • Each homa kunda will have 1 priest and 11 devotees, that are fluent in chanting Sri Rudram.
  • The intent of the event is to maximize participation of devotees to do the homam. All those doing the homam are required to chant to rudram fluently and commit to 90-day sadhana that includes purity of body and mind and entails daily prayers, participation in service, and dietary principles.
  • The first day of the program opens with Ganapathi and Navagraha Homams (for removal of all obstacles and the auspicious alignment of planetary forces and energies) as is traditionally done at the beginning of all great pujas, yajnas etc.
  • Regular daily program will consist of ekadasa (11-times) rudra japam along with abhishekam and homam by 121 devotees and 15 priests.
  • Each day event in the morning will be 5-6 hours; 8 AM to 1 PM followed by lunch prasadam. Evening program will have abhishekam, archana (worship), along with bhajans, satsang, and discourse by Her Holiness Amma Sri Karunamayi.
  • Each day event in the morning will be 5-6 hours; 8 AM to 1 PM followed by lunch prasadam.
  • Grand finale will be on day 11 only with the morning program, purnahuti, and felicitations expected to conclude by 3 PM.
