It is a unique blessing to be part of an Ati Rudra Maha Yajna. Everyone is welcome! It is even more of a blessing if one is able to actively participate in the organization of the Ati Rudra Maha Yajna. Sponsoring the event at any level and/or contributing towards specific items that are to be used in the Yajnam will bring immense blessings, peace, and good health to the individuals and families of the sponsors and the contributors. The sponsors at various levels will also receive a blessed prasadam from Amma at the conclusion of the Maha Yajna. We will update this page with the details of the prasadam for the different sponsorship levels in the weeks ahead. If you would like to sponsor/contribute by check, please follow the directions at the bottom of the page. (or Click Here)

Please Note SRIM center is tax exempt under Section 501 (c) (3) of the Internal Revenue Service as a non-profit religious organization (Tax ID: 38-3739559). Donations to SRIM center are tax deductible to the full extent permitted by law.
